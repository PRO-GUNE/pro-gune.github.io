RoboCon 2023 - International Robotics Competition

RoboCon is an international robotics competition that is held annually by the Asia-Pacific Broadcasting Union (ABU). The competition is open to teams from ABU member countries, which are allowed to send one team each.The competition is usually held in August or September, and the host country is responsible for organizing the event. The competition is open to university students, and the teams are required to design and build a robot that can complete a specific task. The task of RoboCon 2023 was to pick up rings stacked on the floor and shoot it at poles situated in the arena. The competition is judged by a panel of experts, and the winning team receives a trophy and a cash prize.
